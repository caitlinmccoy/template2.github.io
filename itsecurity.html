Recent Projects

1. Segregation of Duties Design and Build

Collaborated with the security work stream as part of a Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 implementation for a worldwide security products manufacturer. Assisted in preparing this organization for User Acceptance Testing (UAT) on a tight timeline to ensure that security is in a good state going into UAT. Using FastPath as the primary tool through out the engagement, a ruleset was developed to identify SoD conflicts based on the major risks for this organization. Facilitated requirements gathering discussions with business process owners from manufacturing, supply chain, finance, and sales order management, and used the finalized ruleset to identify potential conflicts based on the roles and job responsibilities within these four primary processes. Ran numerous FastPath assessments during the iterative process of establishing the user listing, remediating conflicts, and adding rules to the ruleset. Utilized the reports generated from FastPath to analyze the conflicts that exist to continually remediate conflicts at both the role level and the user level. Learn more.

2. Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing

Executed a vulnerability assessment for an insurance company to identify security deficiencies, to identify areas of strength and weakness, to develop a course of action to correct vulnerabilities and to mitigate associated risks. Performed internal and external vulnerability scans and validated each of the vulnerabilities through the use of different Linux modules. Leveraged insecure access controls and configurations to obtain and decrypt valid Domain Administrator credentials resulting in the compromise of the enterprise domain. Learn more.
